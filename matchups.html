Zangief

Tips

  • Stay outside his lows and throw range
  • If close, jump back to maintain your position
  • Focus on safe pressure and punishes
  • Don't use TAP for direct hits.
  • Avoid super for chip unless it kills
  • On your wake-up, you often have to deal with a 50/50 mixup (meaty or 360).

    • Replays

  • Gunze (Zangief) vs. Tsuji (Boxer)
  • Kakki (Zangief) vs. Tsuji (Boxer)

    • Pressure

    - Pressure or find openings with dash low and 5HP
    - If Zangief blocks your jump-Ins, you should immediately jump back. Use j.MP > J.LK/j.MK to bait a reversal.

    Whiff punishes

    He may use preemptive LP and lows to counter dashes. Try to whiff punish, but watch out… you might trade or get caught by a sudden Lariat

    - Punish lows : Dash low LP
    - Punish lights : 2MP or 5HP

    Punishing Lariat

    He may use preemptive lariat to counter dashes. Whiff punish depending on the range:

    Long Range Mid Range* Close Range
    - Dash low - Dash low
    - Ground Upper
    - Super     		- Ground Upper
    - 2MK or 2HK
    - Headbutt
    - Super

    * Be mindful of mid-range spacing, if you're slightly too close, Lariat can hit your dash.

    Using Super: Holding K makes the first hit (Upper) invincible and beats his lariat

    Honda

    Tips

  • Keep your distance and life lead
  • Don't stay too close, avoid his Oichio
  • Patience and defensive playstyle
  • Learn how to punish headbutts and butt-drops.

    • known bug: After getting hit by a 2-hit combo while crouching, Honda will automatically stand up.

    Replays

  • GMC - Tsuji (Boxer) vs. Kusumondo (E.Honda)
  • XSB2022 - FT5 Session : Bazoukha vs Milanea
  • Kusumondo (E.Honda) vs. Makki-7 (Boxer)

    • Oichio

    ⚠️ Oichio beats throw, meaty, close TAP and super. Don’t stick to him too much.

    Sumo Headbutt

    At distance, counter sumo headbutt with 5LP, 2MP or j.back HP/HK. You can also jump back to avoid it then throw.

    Butt-drop

    Charge headbutt, wait for the landing then punish :
    - LK/MK butt-drop: Headbutt LP
    - HK butt-drop: Headbutt or back J.MP

    Hands-slap

    Counter with headbutt, 2MP or Super during his short recovery

    Super Punish

    Use your reversal super to punish his own. Also works with HP dash straight.

    Ken

    Tips

  • Can’t be safe-jumped (HP shoryuken)
  • Avoid his Knee bash mixups
  • Try to bait and punish whiffed DPs
  • Learn how to play around his projectiles
  • Super shifts the match pace, build meter !
  • Change your timing offense to be less predictable.

    • Replays

  • Tsuji (Boxer) vs. Aniken (Ken)
  • Mattsun (Ken) vs Tsuji (Boxer)
  • Goku12 (Ken) VS Bazoukha (Boxer)

    • Shoryuken

    Players often use light DPs to catch dashes. Since DPs knock down, they set up strong pressure and mixups.

    Whiff punish with dash low, Upper or Straight. Then take advantage of it for meter build. No charge ? Punish with throw or 5HP

    LP Shoryuken: Don't react too quickly, it stays active for a short time.

    Meter build: On his knockdown, you can whiff Dash low > Headbutt to build meter (corner only)

    ⚠️ You can't safe jump against Ken's HP Shoryuken.

    Super punish

    This is the maximum range where you can use Super to punish his projectiles.

    How to play around projectiles

    At certain ranges, Ken can trap you for jumping or headbutt through fireballs.

  • Know the right range to use headbutt or super.
  • At a safe distance, without charge: neutral jHP
  • 5HP or Straight to catch Ken’s fireball startup.
  • Block and back off to create space.

    • Knee bash throw loop

    Try to avoid this situation.
    After a jump HP into air Tatsu, his options are:

  • Throw (knee bash) and repeat the sequence
  • Follow up with a low
  • Delay an attack to beat your throw/reversal.
  • Cross-down when you’re in corner

    • Air Tastu

    Air Tatsu can sometimes be used to skip neutral and set up his knee bash throw loop. Depending on the range, punish with headbutt or avoid with Upper (which can be followed by Super).

    Old Ken

    Key differences:

  • Fireballs are 1F faster (startup & recovery)
  • Shoryukens are invincible until their peak
  • Higher priority and damage on normals
  • No Crazy Kicks and Knee Bash Grab
  • No Air Throw
  • Cannot Tech Throws

    • Chun-Li

    Tips

  • Tough matchup, she's hard to approach.
  • She has safe and long reach normals
  • Has many tools to stop boxer dashes.
  • Predict and punish her fireballs
  • Build meter
  • Be careful to her forward walk > Super

    • Replays

  • GMC - Tsuji (Boxer) vs. Otochun (Chun-Li)
  • Super Street Fighter 2 Turbo - Abdel [Boxer] vs znoopyglobal [Chun-Li]

    • Lightning Legs

    ⚠️ Hard to whiff punish since she can quickly stop or repeat it continuously.

  • Invul. with high priority, beats most attacks.
  • Short recovery; she can follow up with Upkicks.
  • On block, punish with Super.

    • Neck Breaker (3HK)

  • Hits mid, also low if deep enough.
  • Airborne frame 1, avoiding throws and reversals.
  • Safe (+8)
  • A well-timed neck breaker leaves you no choice but to block on wake-up

    • Fake crossup

    2HK > Upper LK > j.HK
    This setup makes Chun's reversal upkicks either whiff or get hit by J.HK; in the corner, she always gets hit

    Cammy

    Tips

  • Keep your distance and life lead.
  • Avoid TAP: loses to Drill and Cannon Spike.
  • Her 2MK can stop you from getting in.
  • Counter her 2MK using 2HK (may traade)
  • Use jLK safe jump after a throw
  • Punish Hooligan with back jHP or jMP

    • Replays

  • EVD [Cammy] vs SuperrV [Boxer]
  • hassasin10 [Cammy] vs Digitalinfamy [Boxer]
  • EvilDindon [Cammy] vs New.Bazel [Boxer]

    • Spiral Arrow (Drill)

    Can be blocked while standing. Unsafe, punish from any range with a reversal dash low LP or a regular Super.

    Cammy's 2MK

    Her main poke, great at stopping boxer’s normals and dashes. Be careful, it can be canceled on block with Hooligan Throw. Try to counter it or trade with 2HK. Back off If you have the life lead. On block, be ready to punish a potential Hooligan or any other approaches.

    Hooligan Throw

    Punish it with any anti-airs or back jMP or jHP. Using back jump is a great choice since it creates space and lets you keep your charge.

    Avoid throw left/right mixups

    You can’t use left/right mixups after a thow because she recovers differently from other characters. Instead Hold (9) to follow up with a jLK safe jump.

    Unreliable crossup

    Cammy’s jLK crossups are unreliable on Boxer. Depending on boxer side and position, it can hit, trade or whiff. ==> Credits and image : Vileisland

    Fei-Long

    Tips

  • Avoid Chicken Wing range : walk/jump back.
  • Learn how to handle Rekka and Chicken.
  • Be careful about his crossdown post-throw.
  • Round start : 2MK or 5LP (counter rekka)

    • Replays

  • Afro Legends (Boxer) vs. Airibon (FeiLong)
  • bazouka (Boxer) vs Balcork (Fei Long)
  • Tsuji (Boxer) vs. Youbou (Feilong)

    • Rekka

    Each rekka is unsafe, but he can cancel and repeat it up to 3 times, creating frametraps or mindgames (reversal).
    - The 3rd one is definitely unsafe.
    - All are punishable with Super
    - Whiff punish with 2MK or 5LP

    Chicken Wing

    Allows him to quickly get in, set up mixups (throw, flamekick, low or overhead), or land hit confirms.
    - Safe on block, hits high.
    - Strong corner pressure (no pushback)
    - Whiff punish with Headbutt or Upper at range.
