    • Overview

    Balrog (Boxer) is a top-tier character with high damage, great pokes, fast specials, and a wide throw range. His powerful super also makes him a threat, but without a full meter, he struggles against zoning and okizeme.

    3. Throw

    Use throw MP for its better range.
    On whiff, st.MP comes out and works as an anti-air.

    Throw setups

    2LP,2LP > throw (or mix with headbutt)
    2MP or 2MK > micro walk > throw
    On crouch: whiff Ground Upper > throw

    Throw loop mixups

    After a throw, mix by staying in front or go behind. Then, confirm or tick throw and repeat.
    Fake going behind, then use 2HK to bait a button press (looses to invincible reversal)

    Throw > Safe Jump

    Hold (9) during throw for an auto-timed safe jump. Works midscreen against Boxer and FeiLong with jHP, also against Cammy with jLK.

    Throw > Meaty combos

    Only on Hawk, Sagat, Gief, Boxer, Honda
    - Throw > Meaty 2MK > 2HK
    - Throw > Meaty 2MK > 5HP

    ⚠️ Mixup timing varies by character, you should practice it for consistency.

    4. Super - Crazy Buffalo

    Invincible startup, comes fast, breaks through projectiles, and deals high damage. It's unsafe from a distance, leaving a gap. Always unsafe against Hawk, Gief and Honda.

  • Quicker inputs: [1],3,1,3+P/K.
  • Variations during super: Straights (P) or Upper (K)

    • Super usages: Punish unsafe moves and projectiles, chip kills, wake-up reversals, and combo extensions with 2MK link or renda cancel.

    Notes: Upper (K) whiffs against crouchers. Hold (P) to only use straights. You can also end with headbutt to keep pressure, but it’s risky since it can be unsafe.

    5. Turn Around Punch (TAP)

    Hold then release PPP or KKK
    TAP has 8 levels, increasing with hold time. Higher levels boost speed, range, damage, and blockstun.

  • Start with level 2: Hold TAP before the round start
  • Hold PPP/KKK during a special then release.
  • TAP and follow with a special (and vice versa)

    • TAP lets you quickly close distance, find trades, sets up frametraps, meaties, and build meter. It's useful against zoners and deals high damage or reward.

    More infos: Fighter101 - SSF2T Balrog TAP guide

    6. Charge management

    Quickly chain two charge specials to improve mobility, build meter, find openings, and more.

    Headbutt > Dash

    Hold (7) to Headbutt, then go (3 or 6) to dash

    Dash > Headbutt

    Dash by holding (3), then go (9) for headbutt.

    Upper LK > Super

    Upper LK, then quickly input 4,6+P/K.

    Headbutt > Super

    Hold (7) to Headbutt, then quickly input 4,6+P/K.
