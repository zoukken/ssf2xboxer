# Ken
* Try to bait and whiff punish Shoryuken
* Avoid his Knee bash setups and mixups
* Build meter when possible
* Can’t be safe-jumped (reversal heavy DP)
* Be careful with Ken’s super, it recovers quickly
* Block/Whiff: Super and Heavy DP can switch sides

### Shoryuken
<video data-videojs data-src="media/videos/matchups/ken/Ken_Shoryuken.mp4"
class="video-js vjs-theme-city"></video>

* Players often use light DPs to catch dashes.
* Since DPs knock down, they set up strong pressure and mixups.
* Whiff punish with dash low, Upper or Straight.
* Then take advantage of it for meter build.
* No charge ? Punish with throw or 5HP

#### Notes 
* LP Shoryuken: Don't react too quickly, it stays active for a short time.
* Meter build: On his knockdown, you can whiff Dash low > Headbutt to build meter (corneronly)
* ⚠️ You can't safe jump against Ken's HP Shoryuken.

### Play around projectiles
<video data-videojs data-src="media/videos/matchups/ken/Ken_Projectiles.mp4"
class="video-js vjs-theme-city"></video>

* At certain ranges, Ken can trap you for jumping or headbutt through fireballs.
* Know the right range to use headbutt or super.
* At a safe distance, without charge: neutral jHP
* 5HP or Straight to catch Ken’s fireball startup.
* Block and back off to create space.

### Knee bash throw loop
<video data-videojs data-src="media/videos/matchups/ken/Ken_KneeBashLoop.mp4"
class="video-js vjs-theme-city"></video>

* Avoid this situation. After a jump HP > air Tatsu, he has many options:
* Throw (knee bash) and repeat the sequence
* Follow up with a low
* Delay an attack to beat your throw/reversal.
* Cross-down when you’re in corner

### Air Tastu
<video data-videojs data-src="media/videos/matchups/ken/Ken_AirTatsu.mp4"
class="video-js vjs-theme-city"></video>

* Air Tatsu are often used to skip neutral and sets up knee-bash throw loop.
* Depending on the range, punish with headbutt or avoid with Upper (which can be followed by Super).

### Old Ken

* Fireballs are 1F faster
* Shoryukens are invincible until their peak
* Higher priority and damage on normals
* No Crazy Kicks and Knee Bash Grab
* No Air Throw
* Cannot Tech Throws
