# Feilong

* Play outside chicken wing range
* Be aware of his frametraps/mixups with rekka or chicken wing
* Round start : 2MK or 5LP (counter rekka)
* Be cautious of his cross-down post throw

### Rekka
<video data-videojs data-src="media/videos/matchups/feilong/Rekka.mp4"
class="video-js vjs-theme-city"></video>

Fei Long can perform up to 3 Rekkas and stop after the 1st or 2nd to set up frametraps (Super or Flame Kick). He can also delay each Rekka for additional frametraps. The 3rd one is unsafe.

* Whiff‑punish with 2MK or 5LP
* Each rekka is punishable by Super


### Chicken Wing
<video data-videojs data-src="media/videos/matchups/feilong/ChickenWing.mp4"
class="video-js vjs-theme-city"></video>

Chicken Wing allows him to close-in and set up mixups (throw, reversal, high/low), or land hit confirms.
* Safe on block, hits high.
* Strong corner pressure (no pushback)
* Punish with Headbutt or Upper.
