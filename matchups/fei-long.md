# Feilong

* Play outside chicken wing range
* Be aware of his frametraps/mixups with rekka or chicken wing
* Round start : 2MK or 5LP (counter rekka)
* Be cautious of his cross-down after he throws you

### Rekka
<video data-videojs data-src="media/videos/matchups/feilong/Rekka.mp4"
class="video-js vjs-theme-city"></video>

* Each rekka is unsafe, but he can chain up to three to create frametraps or reversal mindgames.
  * The 3rd one is definitely unsafe.
  * All are punishable with Super
  * Whiff punish with 2MK or 5LP

### Chicken Wing
<video data-videojs data-src="media/videos/matchups/feilong/ChickenWing.mp4"
class="video-js vjs-theme-city"></video>

* Allows him to quickly get in, set up mixups (throw, flamekick, low or overhead), or land hit confirms.
  * Safe on block, hits high.
  * Strong corner pressure (no pushback)
  * Whiff punish with Headbutt or Upper at range.
