# HONDA

* Defensive play.
* Stay at distance, outside his oichio range
* Try keeping the life lead
* Punish sumo headbutts with LP, 2MP or back j.HK
* Punish butt-drop: headbutt or back j.MP
* Avoid obvious thick throws (loose to oicho)
* Make sure to maintain pressure with true blockstring, or commit with headbutt

Useful bug on Honda : After getting a 2-hit combo while crouching, Honda will automatically stand up, making ground or jump-in confirms easier.
