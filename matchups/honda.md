# HONDA

* Defensive play. Stay at distance.
* Try keeping the life lead, if so, don’t approach.
* Whiff punish sumo headbutts and butt-drop
* Meaty and throw are risky (loose to oicho)
* Make sure to use long blockstrings to stay outside his throw range

## Oichio
<video data-videojs data-src="media/videos/matchups/honda/Honda_Oichio.mp4"
class="video-js vjs-theme-city"></video>

* Oichio beats your throw and meaties, and it also punishes close TAP (lvl2+) and Super.
* Don’t stick to him, using tick throw is risky
* Always use long blockstrings (ex: 3 jabs) to avoid Oichio

## 2‑Hit Auto‑Stand Bug
After getting a 2-hit combo while crouching, Honda will automatically stand up, making ground or jump-in confirms easier.
