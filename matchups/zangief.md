# ZANGIEF

## Overview

* Defensive play
* Stay at distance (outside his lows and throw range)
* If too close, jump back LK to maintain spacing
* On Knockdown, expect a meaty or throw mixup
* Avoid using super on block, unless it kills
* Don’t be too predictable, vary your timing (delay)
* Try to read opponent's habits

### Lariat punishes
Watch out for preemptive Lariat or Lariat variations (back or forward) to counter your dashes.
| Long-Range | Mid-Range | Close-Range |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| - Dash low | - Dash low<br>- Ground Upper<br>- Super | - Ground Upper<br>- 2MK or 2HK<br>- Headbutt<br>- Super |

**_Mid range_** — Be mindful of spacing; if you're too close, Lariat can catch your dash.
**_Super_** — Hold **K** (Upper) to make it invincible and punish Lariat.
