# Cammy

* Avoid TAP: loses to Drill and Cannon Spike.
* Her 2MK can stop you from getting in.
* Counter her 2MK using 2HK (may trade)
* Use jLK safe jump after a throw
* Punish Hooligan with back jHP or jMP

### Spiral Arrow (Drill)
<video data-videojs data-src="media/videos/matchups/cammy/SipralArrow.mp4"
class="video-js vjs-theme-city"></video>

* Can be blocked while standing.
* Safe depending on range.
* Unsafe if you have meter, punish from any range with a or a regular Super.

### Cammy's 2MK
<video data-videojs data-src="media/videos/matchups/cammy/Cammy2MK.mp4"
class="video-js vjs-theme-city"></video>

* Her main poke, great at stopping boxer’s normals and dashes. 
* Be careful, it can be canceled on block with Hooligan Throw.
* Try to counter it or trade with 2HK.
* Back off If you have the life lead.
* On block, be ready to punish a potential Hooligan or any other approaches.

### Hooligan Throw
<video data-videojs data-src="media/videos/matchups/cammy/HooliganThrow.mp4"
class="video-js vjs-theme-city"></video>

* Comes fast, often used at close/mid range.
* Punish with any anti-air or back j.MP or j.HP.
* Using back jump is a great choice since it creates space and lets you keep your charge.

### Throw mixups
<video data-videojs data-src="media/videos/matchups/cammy/ThrowSafeJump.mp4"
class="video-js vjs-theme-city"></video>

* Her recovery after throw is different from other characters, be careful before using left/right mixups.
* You can also hold (9) during throw to get an auto-timed j.LK safe jump.

### Cammy’s unreliable crossup
<img class="charbg" style="width:100%;" src="media/videos/matchups/cammy/Crossup.png">

* Cammy’s j.LK crossup is difficult to perform on Boxer and rarely works consistently.
* Depending on boxer side and position, it can either hit, trade or whiff.
