    • 1. Hit confirms

    2LP,2LP is a chain combo, it requires timing to do a link so you can cancel it. 2LK~2LK, is a link by default.

    Normal confirms

    5LP/2LP > Dash
    2LK > Dash
    2MP > Dash
    2MK > 2MP > Dash

    Standing Opponent

    2LP, 2LP, 5LP > Dash
    2MK > 5LP > Dash

    2. Jump-Ins

    You need to hit deep (center of body) to confirm a jump-in, otherwise it won't combo.

    Jump-Ins

    j.HP or j.MP > 5HP
    j.HP > 5MK > Dash
    j.HP > 2MP > Dash
    j.HP > 2MK > 2MP > Dash

    Standing Opponent

    j.HK, 2LP > 5LP > Dash

    3. Super

    Super link

    2MK > Super
    2MK, 2MK > Super

    Super cancel

    2MK > 2MP > Super
    2MK > 5MK > Super

    Renda cancel (Super chain cancel)

    2LP,2LP,2LP > Super
    Inputs: [1] 2LP,2LP > 321LP > 6LP~MP
    2LP,2LP,5LP > Super
    Inputs: [1]LP,LP > 3214LP > 6LP~MP

    ✅ For a better success with renda cancel, avoid hitting neutral when sliding [1] → 321
