    • Normal confirms

    Normal confirms

    5LP/2LP > Dash
    2LK > Dash
    2MP > Dash
    2MK > 2MP > Dash

    Standing Opponent

    2LP, 2LP, 5LP > Dash
    2MK > 5LP > Dash

    Practice your timing with the 2LP, 2LP to make it a link, not a chain, so you can cancel it. Otherwise, just use 2LK, 2LK instead, as it's always a link.

    Jump-Ins

    You need to hit deep (center of body) to confirm a jump-in with a crouch attack, otherwise it won't combo.

    Jump-Ins

    j.HP > 5HP
    j.HP > 5MK > Dash
    j.HP > 2MP > Dash
    j.HP > 2MK > 2MP > Dash

    Standing Opponent

    j.HK, 2LP > 5LP > Dash

    Super

    Super link

    2MK > Super
    2MK, 2MK > Super

    Renda cancel

    2LP > Super
    Inputs: [1] > 321LP > 6LP~MP
    2LP,2LP,5LP > Super
    Inputs: [1]LP,LP > 3214LP > 6LP~MP

    Renda Cancel Tips : Try to not reach neutral while quickly going from [1] to 321 motion (to be confirmed)
