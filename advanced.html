    • Meter build

    Super is essential in hard matchups. Build meter whenever possible by whiffing special moves, especially after a knockdown.

    Midscreen - Knockdown

    Ground Upper --> Headbutt
    Ground Upper --> TAP > Headbutt LP
    Dash low --> TAP
    Dash low --> Headbutt LP
    Headbutt --> TAP > Upper
    Headbutt --> Ground Upper > Headbutt
    Super --> TAP > Upper
    Super --> Upper > Headbutt
    Super --> Headbutt > Upper

    Corner - Knockdown

    Ground Upper --> TAP / Headbutt
    Headbutt --> TAP / Upper
    Dash low --> TAP / Headbutt
    Super --> TAP / Upper / Headbutt

    Frametraps

    Prevent opponents from mashing throws or buttons while blocking. Punish their mash attempts and hit confirm when possible... or condition them with tick throws. Keep in mind that frametraps lose to reversals.

    Here are frametrap examples :
    ⚠️ They may vary depending on the matchup

    Ground

    5MK > 2MK / 2MP
    2MK > 2MK / 2HK / 5HP
    2MP > 5HP / 2MK
    2LP,2LP > 2MP / 2HK / Headbutt
    2LP,2LP,2LP > 2MP
    Dash low LP > 5HP / Headbutt (close range)

    Jump-in

    j.MP > 2HK / 2MP

    TAP

    lvl 3+ > Super / Headbutt (need proper spacing)

    Safe jumps

    ⚠️ You can't safe jump against Ken's HP Shoryuken.

    Midscreen

    Dash low > 2LP > j.HP
    2HK > 2LP > j.HP
    Headbutt > Upper > 2LP > j.HP
    Ground Upper > TAP lvl1 > j.HP

    Corner

    Dash low > j.HP
    Headbutt > 2LP > j.HP
    Ground Upper > 2MK > j.HP

    During throw (midscreen)

    Hold (9) > jHP (Boxer, Feilong)
    Hold (9) > jLK (Cammy)
    ✅ Try learning the jump timing without using those setups to be less predictable.

    Ground Upper

    At mid range, use Ground Upper LK to quickly react to opponents walking forward.

    Ground Upper - Throw setup

    2MP > 2LP > Ground Upper > Throw
    Ground Upper whiffs on crouch, but leaves you close enough for MP throw. Mix with headbutt or super to counter throw attempt.

    Side switch

    - headbutt anti-air > dash low > headbutt HP
    - headbutt > upper > headbutt HP

    Autocorrect Dashes

    while holding your down charge, just hit one button (like HK here) at the moment they go behind you. Useful against Claw.

    Auto-Stand Bug

    A bug makes 2LP act as 5LP.
    Whiff 5LP to store the bug. Then, chain 2LK,2LP while holding back-down.
