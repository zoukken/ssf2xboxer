    • 1. Meter build

    Super is essential in hard matchups. Build meter whenever possible by whiffing special moves, especially after a knockdown.

    Midscreen - Knockdown

    Dash low --> TAP / Headbutt LP
    Ground Upper --> Headbutt
    Ground Upper --> TAP > Headbutt LP
    Headbutt --> TAP > Upper
    Headbutt --> Ground Upper > Headbutt
    Super --> TAP > Upper
    Super --> Upper > Headbutt
    Super --> Headbutt > Upper

    Corner - Knockdown

    Ground Upper --> TAP / Headbutt
    Headbutt --> TAP / Upper
    Super --> TAP / Upper / Headbutt

    2. Frametraps

    Prevent opponents from mashing throws or buttons while blocking. Punish their mash attempts and hit confirm when possible... or condition them with tick throws. Keep in mind that frametraps lose to reversals.

    Ground

    5MK > 2MK / 2MP
    2MK > 2MK / 2HK / 5HP
    2MP > 5HP / 2MK
    2LP,2LP > 2MP / 2HK / Headbutt
    2LP,2LP,2LP > 2MP
    Dash low LP > 5HP / Headbutt (close range)

    Jump-in

    j.MP > 2HK / 2MP

    TAP

    lvl 3+ > Super / Headbutt (need proper spacing)

    ⚠️ Frametraps may vary depending on the matchup

    3. Safe jumps

    ⚠️ You can't safe jump against Ken's HP Shoryuken.

    Midscreen

    Dash low > 2LP > j.HP
    2HK > 2LP > j.HP
    Headbutt > Upper > 2LP > j.HP
    Ground Upper > TAP lvl1 > j.HP

    Corner

    Dash low > j.HP
    Headbutt > 2LP > j.HP
    Ground Upper > 2MK > j.HP

    During throw (midscreen)

    Hold (9) > jHP (Boxer, Feilong)
    Hold (9) > jLK (Cammy)
    ✅ Try learning the jump timing without using those setups to be less predictable.

    4. Ground Upper Mixups

    Ground upper is fast and whiffs on crouch. Leaves you close for MP throw. Use it on crouch or wake-up to mix with throw, meaty, headbutt, or super.

    At mid range, you can also use Ground Upper LK to quickly react to opponents walking forward.

    5. Fuzzy & Instant overhead

    Fuzzy and instant overheads are unsafe on block, only use them to finish the opponent.

    How to

    Instant overhead: j.LK or vertical jump LP/LK
    Fuzzy: j.LK > j.LK

    Setups (all ranges)

    Dash low --> TAP > j.LK
    On crouch: Whiff Ground Upper > j.LK
    Throw --> walk > j.LK (need timing)

    6. Side switch

    Midscreen

    Headbutt anti-air --> Dash low > Headbutt HP
    Headbutt --> Upper > Headbutt HP

    Corner

    Throw > Upper
    Throw > Walk

    7. Dash autocorrect

    Hold your charge and press a button (like HK) as they cross behind you. Works well vs Claw.

    8. Auto-Stand Bug

    A bug makes boxer's 2LP act as 5LP.
    Whiff 5LP to store the bug, then activate it by chaining 2LK → 2LP while crouching.

    9. Walk > Super

    Boxer’s Super charge can be broken into parts, letting you walk for up to 26 frames without losing the input.

    Charge your super, then:
  • Part 1: → (walk up to 15F)
  • Part 2: ← (back 1F)
  • Part 3: → + P (walk up to 11F and press Punch)
